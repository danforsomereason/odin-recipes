Recipe for Disaster


Get me outta here!

Description

If you want to fail at becomming a programmer and waste alot of time, energy, and even money, then this is the recipe for you. Perfect for building frustration and disappointment, this Recipe for Disaster is easy, and best of all, anyone can do it! This recipe will leave a bitter taste in your mouth and have you wondering, What could have been!""

Ingredients

  • Impatience
  • The desire to be a savant
  • Endless tutorials
  • 3 AI Subscriptions

Recipe

  1. Start by wanting all the perks of being a senior developer and discard the desire to start as a beginner.
  2. Gather a list of resources and tools and set aside to rest.
  3. Copy AI-generated code and follow tutorials mindlessly.
  4. Skip the boring parts. You don't need those.
  5. Continually stir self-doubt and comparison to others.
  6. There are very explicit documentation items to learn programming languages, frameworks, and libraries, but resist the urge to read them.
  7. Apply for hundreds of jobs each week, but don't look closely at the job description or tailor your resume, cover letter, and approach to it.
  8. Finally, and I cannot stress this enough, do not work on building things, solving problems, or pushing yourself.
