Recipe for Disaster


Get me outta here!

Ingredients

  • Impatience
  • The desire to be a savant
  • Endless tutorials
  • 3 AI Subscriptions

Recipe

  • Start by wanting all the perks of being a senior developer and discard the desire to start as a beginner.
  • Gather a list of resources and tools and set aside to rest.
  • Copy AI-generated code and follow tutorials mindlessly.
  • Skip the boring parts. You don't need those.
  • Continually stir self-doubt and comparison to others.
  • There are very explicit documentation items to learn programming languages, frameworks, and libraries, but resist the urge to read them.
  • Apply for hundreds of jobs each week, but don't look closely at the job description or tailor your resume, cover letter, and approach to it.
  • Finally, and I cannot stress this enough, do not work on building things, solving problems, or pushing yourself.
